How to Delete Your Account

Grotto Mercs

LateNiteScripts LLC
Last Updated: June 20, 2025
⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game progress, achievements, multiplayer statistics, and in-game purchases will be permanently lost.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All game progress and save data
  • Achievements and statistics
  • Multiplayer match history
  • Friends lists and social connections
  • In-game purchases and virtual items
  • Chat history and communications

Account Deletion Methods

Method 1: In-Game Account Deletion

Step 1: Open Game Settings

  1. Launch Grotto Mercs on your device
  2. Navigate to the main menu
  3. Tap on the "Settings" or "Options" button
[Screenshot: Main menu with Settings button highlighted]

Step 2: Find Account Settings

  1. In the Settings menu, look for "Account" or "Profile"
  2. Tap on "Account Management" or "Account Settings"
[Screenshot: Settings menu with Account option highlighted]

Step 3: Delete Account

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the Account Settings
  2. Tap on "Delete Account" or "Remove My Account"
  3. Read the warning message carefully
  4. Type "DELETE" to confirm (if prompted)
  5. Tap "Confirm Deletion"
[Screenshot: Account settings page with Delete Account button]
[Screenshot: Confirmation dialog with warning message]

Method 2: Platform-Specific Deletion

For iOS/tvOS Users:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device
  2. Scroll down and tap on "Privacy & Security"
  3. Tap on "Apple Advertising"
  4. Reset your Advertising Identifier
  5. Alternatively, delete the app and all its data from Settings → General → iPhone Storage

For Android Users:

  1. Open Settings on your Android device
  2. Go to "Apps" or "Application Manager"
  3. Find and select "Grotto Mercs"
  4. Tap "Storage"
  5. Tap "Clear Data" and "Clear Cache"
  6. Uninstall the app

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account using the methods above, or if you need assistance, please contact our support team:

Email: support@lns.dev

Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - Grotto Mercs"

Please include your username and the platform you're playing on (iOS, tvOS, or Android).

What Happens After Deletion

Immediate Effects:

  • Your account will be immediately deactivated
  • You will be logged out of all devices
  • Other players will no longer see your profile
  • You cannot log back into the deleted account

Within 30 Days:

  • All personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • Backup copies will be removed from our systems
  • Your data cannot be recovered after this period

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account permanently, consider these alternatives:

  • Take a break: Simply uninstall the app without deleting your account
  • Change privacy settings: Adjust what data is shared in the game settings
  • Contact support: We may be able to help resolve any issues you're experiencing
Remember: Account deletion is final. We cannot restore deleted accounts or transfer progress to new accounts.
